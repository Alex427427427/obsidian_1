### Patricia's teaching
Why did the war begin? Because the Atlantian royals could no longer feed their people. 
Why did the war last 10 years? 

Because the knowledge of "who would win" did not converge for 10 years. 

However, that knowledge - that information - was already there long before the start of the war. It existed in the farms, the factories, the mines, the daily choices in the civilians and royals, of both Atlantia and Eurasia. It existed in their technology stacks. It existed in their bureaucracies. It existed in the nuclei of the atoms of their Uranium stockpile. The knowledge of "who would win a war" lay dormant and scattered across the world. 

If the Atlantian elites knew they would lose catastrophically, despite their short surprise advantage, they would never have started the war. And until they knew this truth, they had no reason to surrender. That's why the war lasted 10 years. It took 10 years for the information scattered across the world to converge, through battlefields, to their command centers, and finally to the brain of their Emperor and military strategist. 

Everything is about information, Lucas. In a world where everyone knows all truths about the world, war is impossible to begin. (the frequency domain)
### The Crime of Eurasia
A small nation now in central Eurasia was once occupied by the Atlantian army. The Eurasian army was going to launch an attack to liberate the nation. The local resistance army planned their uprising to coincide. But they received the wrong date for the Eurasian offensive (a month early), and were crushed by the Atlantian army, who commited war crimes against the nation, scorching 90% of the population. the Eurasian army eventually arrived and rescued the surviving girl Patricia. 

The telegrapher that made the typo committed suicide. Patricia visits that grave (brings Lucas after he joins Illuminati) and tells the telegrapher it's not his fault. 

The telegrapher was ordered by Eurasian authorities to deliver the wrong information so that the local resistance could be crushed by the Atlantians first, then the Eurasians could come in to establish their own government after defeating the Atlantians.
